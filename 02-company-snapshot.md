## Operating Model

Gumroad operates with zero full-time employees. As of 2025, the team consists of 14 part-time international contractors (reduced from ~32 in early 2025), typically working 20-35 hours per week. Compensation is a mix of cash and equity (options via Flexile platform at $100M valuation with 10-year exercise windows).

The company runs on a remarkably lean infrastructure:

- No office
- No full-time staff
- Flat organizational structure
- Ershad Kunnakkadan (CEO since Nov 2025) runs operations; Sahil remains Chairman and sets strategic direction
- Financial close and dividend decisions follow a regular board cadence

This model produces exceptional margins but creates specific tax and compliance considerations around contractor classification, reasonable compensation, and accumulated earnings -- all addressed in this analysis.
